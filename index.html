Asset Management

Asset management is the systematic process of developing, operating, maintaining, and disposing of assets cost-effectively. It involves tracking both physical and digital assets to maximize their value and ensure optimal utilization. Organizations across industries, including IT, manufacturing, healthcare, and finance, use asset management to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance decision-making.

Effective asset management includes tracking asset lifecycles, scheduling maintenance, managing inventory, and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. It helps businesses optimize resources, prevent asset downtime, and make informed financial decisions. Modern asset management systems utilize technologies such as RFID, GPS, barcodes, and cloud computing to automate tracking and monitoring.
